AmnioProPlus

Best suited for Chronic & Acute Wounds

Best suited for:

  • Chronic Wounds (e.g., diabetic ulcers, pressure ulcers)
  • Acute Wounds (e.g., surgical wounds, traumatic wounds)

Why?

AmnioProPlus is an advanced biologic with a high concentration of growth factors, promoting tissue regeneration and healing. It is ideal for chronic wounds, such as diabetic ulcers or pressure ulcers, and acute wounds like surgical incisions or traumatic injuries. Its regenerative properties make it suitable for wounds anywhere on the body (arms, legs, back), where faster healing is critical.

Advantages:

  • Supports wound healing through angiogenesis and tissue regeneration
  • Excellent for use on chronic and acute wounds across various body locations
  • Effective in enhancing reepithelialization and tissue growth

AmnioQuadCore

Best suited for Venous Ulcers & Highly Exudative Wounds

Best suited for:

  • Venous Ulcers (legs, arms, back—anywhere with compromised circulation)
  • Highly Exudative Wounds (legs, arms, back)
  • Post-Surgical Wounds (e.g., Mohs procedures, large incisions)

Why?

AmnioQuadCore is optimal for heavily exuding wounds such as venous ulcers or post-surgical wounds. Its four-layer structure offers strong moisture management, making it highly effective in controlling exudate and maintaining a moist healing environment. It's perfect for venous ulcers on the legs or other areas where vascular compromise is present, and post-surgical wounds where infection control and enhanced healing are crucial.

Advantages:

  • Superior moisture absorption, perfect for wounds with high exudate
  • Strong barrier properties, ideal for post-surgical and venous ulcers across the body
  • Excellent for use in highly exudative wounds, regardless of location

AmnioTriCore

Best suited for Moderate-to-Low Exudating Wounds

Best suited for:

  • Moderate-to-Low Exudating Wounds (e.g., partial-thickness burns, surgical incisions)
  • Moderate Surgical or Traumatic Wounds (arms, back, legs, face)

Why?

AmnioTriCore is designed for moderate wound coverage and offers flexibility for repositioning, which is particularly beneficial for wounds in areas that move frequently (like arms and joints). It's suitable for surgical wounds and partial-thickness burns on any part of the body, where a medium level of exudate management is required. Its three-layer structure ensures a good balance between wound protection and ease of use.

Advantages:

  • Flexible and easy to reposition for wound locations that experience movement
  • Ideal for moderate exudating wounds, including surgical and burn wounds
  • Provides effective wound coverage with moderate exudate control

AmnioCore

Best suited for Lower Exudating Wounds

Best suited for:

  • Lower Exudating Wounds (e.g., superficial wounds, minor burns, abrasions)
  • Surgical Incisions (anywhere on the body)

Why?

AmnioCore is designed for wounds with low exudation or superficial wounds that need minimal coverage but still benefit from biologic enhancement. It's a suitable choice for superficial surgical wounds, incisions, or abrasions across various parts of the body, providing sufficient protection while promoting healing. Its simple structure makes it easy to apply and manage in wounds that do not require extensive absorption or strength.

Advantages:

  • Great for managing low-exudating wounds, including surgical incisions and minor trauma
  • Effective for use across all body areas, offering flexible coverage
  • Promotes biologically enhanced healing for more superficial wound types

AmnioCore Pro

Best suited for Full-Thickness Wounds

Best suited for:

  • Full-Thickness Wounds (e.g., large traumatic wounds, deeper ulcers)
  • Chronic Wounds with Slow Healing (e.g., pressure ulcers, diabetic ulcers)

Why?

AmnioCore Pro offers advanced biologic properties for full-thickness wounds and chronic wounds that are difficult to heal. This product is ideal for large traumatic injuries or deep ulcers, particularly those that need strong regenerative support across the body, including the back, arms, and legs. The enhanced structure of AmnioCore Pro provides superior tissue support and scaffold for cell growth, making it an excellent choice for complex or long-standing wounds.

Advantages:

  • Advanced biologic design for full-thickness or complex wounds
  • Promotes faster healing and tissue regeneration in chronic wounds
  • Suitable for large, deep wounds across the body that require robust support
